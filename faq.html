Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the common questions our customers ask about solar systems and our services.

  • Typical residential installations take 1–3 days depending on system size and site conditions. Commercial sites may take longer due to permits and scaling.
  • We provide workmanship warranties (variable by contract) and manufacturer warranties for panels & inverters (typically 10–25 years on panels, 5–10 on inverters).
  • Batteries provide backup during outages and increase self-consumption. If you need uninterrupted power, we recommend a battery as part of a hybrid system.
